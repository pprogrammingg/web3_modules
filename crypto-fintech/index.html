🔐 Cryptography for Blockchains & FinTech

Seven levels — exactly three modules each — theoretical depth paired with hands-on labs. Each module is paced as ~20–25 minute segments (about two hours total reading) plus optional deeper tinkering. Levels 2–7 include one guided build (coral card) per level—shipping small artifacts, not only quizzes.

Who this trains

Backend and protocol engineers who need credible cryptography vocabulary: enough to read specs, review implementations, and collaborate with security teams—without pretending to be a cryptographer. Modules emphasize what breaks in production (API misuse, timing, custody, ZK positioning) as much as textbook definitions.
