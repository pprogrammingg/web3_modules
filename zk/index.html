🧪 Zero-Knowledge for Blockchains

Seven levels — exactly three modules each — focused on proof systems, rollups, and shipping safely. Each module is paced as ~20–25 minute segments (~two hours reading) plus labs. Levels 2–7 include a guided build (jade card) designed as portfolio artifacts—Circom, benchmarks, verifier traces, audits.

Who this trains

Protocol and backend engineers who must read ZK roadmaps, integrate verifiers, or pair with cryptographers—without claiming research credentials. Complements the Cryptography track (primitives & FinTech ops) and protocol tracks like Hyperscale.
